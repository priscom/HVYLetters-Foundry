PRISMATIC

PRISMATIC IS A THREE-DIMENSIONAL TYPEFACE BUILT FROM A STRICT MODULAR GRID AND SHAPED THROUGH PRISMATIC RUSTICATION. EVERY FORM IS CONSTRUCTED AS A RELIEF SHARP EDGES ANGLED CUTS AND FACETS DESIGNED TO WORK WITH REAL LIGHT. IT FUNCTIONS AS A SPATIAL WRITING SYSTEML ETTERFORMS THAT EXIST AS OBJECTS FIRST GRAPHICS SECOND. ORIGINALLY INSPIRED BY A FRAGMENT OF STONE LETTERING ON THE RHEIN UNION HALL IN BERLIN PRISMATIC EXPANDS A HANDFUL OF SURVIVING CHARACTERS INTO A COMPLETE SET. THE TYPEFACE ISNT A LITERAL REVIVAL. INSTEAD IT USES THE MATERIAL LOGIC OF CARVED LETTERFORMS DEPTH SHADOW PRESSURE AND STRUCTURE TO BUILD SOMETHING NEW.
